Welcome to the Zarr Summit

Revolutionizing Open Science on the Cloud

The Zarr Summit Adopter Days convene leaders from major scientific organizations with Zarr developers and implementers for 2 intensive days focused on accelerating the adoption of cloud-native data storage solutions for massive scientific datasets.

From climate modeling to Earth observation to genomics research, Zarr offers unprecedented capabilities for storing and accessing multi-dimensional scientific data in the cloud. These Adopter Days provide a unique opportunity for organizations to learn directly from core developers and share their experiences with Zarr.

Why Your Sponsorship Matters

Zarr is built on community contributions. Your sponsorship enables a bright future for the project, by enabling unprecedented collaboration between major scientific organizations and the core Zarr development team.

About the Zarr Summit Adopter Days

Event Focus

Adopter Days (October 16-17)

Two intensive days bringing together major scientific organizations, data providers, and Zarr implementers to accelerate real-world adoption through:

  • Roundtables with Zarr core developers and steering council
  • Migration strategies and best practices sessions
  • Virtual Zarr implementation guidance
  • Lightning talks from current adopters
  • Case study presentations from successful implementations

Participants

Our summit brings together an incredible diverse range of organizations, including:

  • Governmental Agencies: NASA, ESA, EUMETSAT, and more
  • Research Institutions: Climate prediction centers, genomics labs, and more
  • Technology Leaders: CloudFerro, NVIDIA, Google, and more
  • Zarr Core Team: Leading developers and steering council members

Expected Outcomes

The Adopter Days will provide a groundwork for future innovation:

  • Best practices guidance for petabyte-scale deployments
  • Roadmap for future Zarr development and adopter support
